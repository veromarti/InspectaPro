# Business Interpretation

## 1. Purpose of the System

InspectaPro is a digital platform designed to manage the complete lifecycle of inspections within a company.

The system allows organizations to define inspection types, assign technicians, execute inspections, and validate results in a structured and controlled way.

Its purpose is to centralize inspection processes and ensure traceability, accountability, and operational efficiency.

## 2. Core Business Concept

The core business concept is inspection management.

An inspection is a structured activity assigned to a technician, based on a predefined inspection type, and validated by an administrator.

Each inspection follows a lifecycle:

- Creation
- Assignment
- Execution
- Validation
- Closure
## 3. Business Roles

The business operates around three main roles:

- Company Administrator → Manages inspections and system configuration
- Technician → Executes inspections in the field
- Company → Owns the data and operational context
## 4. Business Value

The system provides value by:

- Improving operational control
- Reducing manual processes
- Increasing transparency in inspection results
- Ensuring proper validation before closing inspections
